3. Information We Collect

When you submit a form or contact us through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name — to identify and address you properly
  • Phone Number — to contact you by phone or SMS regarding your request
  • Email Address — to follow up on your estimate or service inquiry
  • Property Address — to understand your roofing project location
  • Project Details / Message — any information you provide about your roofing needs
  • Form Submission Information — including the type of service requested and any additional notes you provide
  • SMS Consent Status — whether you have opted in to receive SMS communications from us

We may also collect general website usage information (such as browser type and page visits) through standard web technologies for analytics purposes. This information does not identify you personally.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide for the following purposes:

  • Responding to roofing estimate requests submitted through our website
  • Scheduling appointments and coordinating service visits
  • Providing updates on your roofing project or service inquiry
  • Following up with you about your submitted request
  • Communicating via phone, email, or SMS in connection with your request
  • Sending promotional messages only if you have separately opted in to receive promotional communications

We do not use your information for any purpose that is unrelated to the services we provide or that you have not consented to.

5. SMS / Text Message Communications

Consent to Receive SMS Messages

By submitting our website form and checking the required SMS consent checkbox, you agree to receive SMS (text) messages from DAMARTINEZ Roofing. These messages may include:

  • Confirmation of your estimate request
  • Appointment scheduling and confirmation messages
  • Service updates related to your roofing project
  • Follow-up communications regarding your inquiry

If you separately opted in to promotional communications, we may also send promotional messages about our roofing services.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on the nature of your request and the stage of your project.

Rates

Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier and plan.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from DAMARTINEZ Roofing.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for help, or contact us directly at 609-964-8765 or damartinezroofingllc@gmail.com.

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We take your privacy seriously. The following outlines how we handle data sharing:

  • We do not sell your personal information to any third party for any purpose.

  • We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or other legal process.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, complete any agreed-upon services, and maintain records for standard business purposes. You may contact us at any time to request that we delete your information, subject to any legal retention obligations.
